Yi Liu

Hola! I'm a PhD Candidate at UGent AiRO-imec, where I am interested in promoting the robots integrate into human's daily life smarter and safer. My research focuses on robotic learning, which enables robots to intelligently interact with the world in which humans exist. Recently, I've been interested in how robots can interact with soft body more smoothly.


Yi Liu is a PhD Candidate (3rd-year) at UGent AiRO-imec working on robotics and reinforcement learning, supervised by Francis wyffels. He received his Bachelor in Automation in 2018 and his M.Eng in Control Engineering in 2021 at Guangdong University of Tech. He is interested in building control systems and algorithms that enable robots to interact with the human world more intelligently and safely. He is also interested in transfer learning, imitation learning, classical control algorithms, etc. His previous research includes human-robot bilateral teleoperation, reinforcement learning sim2real, robotic contact with soft body (organs) and surgical robot positioning technology.
